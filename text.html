Microwave Foul Medames

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of fava beans (the 5 EGP kind)
  • 1/2 lemon (or whole if you're bold)
  • 1 garlic clove (or 3 to repel vampires)
  • Cumin (the magic powder)
  • Olive oil (if you're fancy)
  • Salt (until you stop crying about your empty wallet)

How I Do It:

  1. Open can without cutting yourself (hardest step)
  2. Dump beans with juice into microwave-safe bowl
  3. Nuke for 2 minutes (stand back - it splatters!)
  4. Mash roughly with fork (lumps add character)
  5. Add lemon juice, crushed garlic, cumin, salt
  6. Drown in oil like it's your ex's heart
  7. Eat with stale bread or straight from bowl

Pro Tip: Add chopped tomatoes if you find extra 3 EGP. Tastes best at 3am while questioning life choices.

Stinky Potato Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes (the sad ones from your fridge)
  • 2 eggs (if you can afford them this week)
  • 1 onion (the bigger the better)
  • 3 garlic cloves (for that lasting aroma)
  • Salt & pepper (to pretend you're a chef)
  • Oil (not too much, we're not millionaires)

How I Do It:

  1. Peel potatoes (or don't, I'm not your mom)
  2. Cut into small cubes (smaller = cooks faster)
  3. Heat oil in pan (medium heat unless you like smoke)
  4. Fry potatoes until kinda golden (who has time for perfect?)
  5. Add chopped onion and garlic (the stinkier the better)
  6. Crack eggs directly into the mess
  7. Scramble everything together like your life
  8. Eat with bread (or your hands, no judgment)

Granny's Wisdom: Add chili flakes if you want to feel something. Tastes best eaten straight from the pan at 2am.

Poor Man's Koshari

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (the cheapest you can find)
  • 1/2 cup lentils (any color, we're not picky)
  • 1 cup pasta (any shape, it all becomes mush)
  • 2 large onions (for crying and eating)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minimum)
  • Tomatoes (canned or fresh, whatever's cheaper)
  • Vinegar & chili sauce (for the brave)
  • Oil (for frying and regretting life choices)

How I Do It:

  1. Cook lentils until mushy (about 20 mins of boredom)
  2. Cook rice and pasta separately (or together if lazy)
  3. Slice onions and fry until crispy (the actual hard part)
  4. Make tomato sauce: cook tomatoes with crushed garlic
  5. Mix rice, lentils and pasta in big bowl (the chaos begins)
  6. Top with tomato sauce and crispy onions
  7. Drown in vinegar and chili sauce (like your sorrows)
  8. Eat until you can't move (then eat some more)

Street Vendor Secret: Missing ingredients? Use what you have and call it "fusion koshari". Tastes best shared with friends who'll help with dishes.
